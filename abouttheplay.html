Brief summary

"Fahrenheit 451 tells the story of Guy Montag and his transformation from a book-burning fireman to a book-reading rebel. Montag lives in an oppressive society that attempts to eliminate all sources of complexity, contradiction, and confusion to ensure uncomplicated happiness for all its citizens." (Sparknotes). Accompany Guy on his journey to free himself and the people from the awful society they currrently live in.
